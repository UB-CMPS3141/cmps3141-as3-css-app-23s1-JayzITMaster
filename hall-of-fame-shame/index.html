Introduction

The website I've chosen to critique about is www.footlocker.com. I will also speak about what I like about the webpage, what I wish the changes could have been and what if it was done differently.

I Like

  • Clean and Attractive can create a positive first impression.
  • Easy Navigation, its helpful to find items that users are looking for.
  • All images are in High-resolution.
  • Detailed Product Information.
  • Robust search functionality.

I Wish

  • Visual Product Filters.
  • Improve better view for mobile phones.
  • Wishlist and Shopping Cart Features.
  • Lack of showing security for transaction.
  • Offer real-time customer support through live chat or chatbots.

What if

  • Use visual product filters, such as color switches, so its easier for users to narrow down their choice of browsing products.
  • Utilizing a web design framework or CSS library like Bootstrap, that provides pre-built responsive components and grids for a mobile-friendly layout.
  • Enable users to save items in a wishlist and easily manage their shopping cart. Offer options to save for later and receive notifications for restocked or discounted items.
  • Display trust signals, such as SSL certificates and secure payment logos, to reasure users that their personal and financial information is safe.
  • Incorporate a live-chat or a chatbot platform that is programmed for its objectives and scope, selecting a framework that suits the needs of the company's website and to design the chatbot's converstional flow.
